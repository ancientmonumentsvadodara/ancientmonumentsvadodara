Vadodara aslo known as Baroda, Gujarat's third largest city, is considered the state's cultural capital.Vadodara is situated on the Vishwamitri River and is the administrative headquarters of Vadodara District. The city is mixture of many eras and its reputation as "Cultural Capital" dates largely from the late 19th c, when it was the capital of the pricely state of Vadodara.
Baroda has a rich historical background and this website will guide you about the Top five Monuments of Vadodara.
